HOLA!


I was born and raised in Stockholm, add some amazing roots from Chile. During most of my 20s I travelled and lived abroad, roaming the world calmly and tranquilly.

Love design in all areas and visiting so many beautiful countries with their handicrafts reflecting the cultural traditions, made me come up with a business idea - textile and jewellery design.

Finally settling down in my hometown and worked as a teacher for many years, introducing didactical solutions for my students and loved how I could implement design, art and aestethics into the lessons.

Times change and also did I. With a huge interest in the web development business and how creative you can be, I decided to re-train myself to become a Frontend Developer.
And here I am, a Frontend Developer student. Don't hesitate to contact me.
