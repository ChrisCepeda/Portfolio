Born and based in Stockholm, add some amazing roots from Chile. During most of my 20s I travelled and lived abroad, roaming the world calmly and tranquilly.

Love design in all areas and visiting different continents introduced me to a world of handicrafts reflecting cultural traditions. That inspired me to start a small business designing clothes and jewellery from India and Indonesia.

Settling down in my hometown I decided to work as a teacher, changing the textile design into didactical solutions for my students and lo ved how I could implement design, art and aestethics into my lessons.

Times changed and so did I. With a huge interest in the web development industry I decided to re-train myself to become a Frontend Developer.

So at the moment I am retraining myself at Hyper Island to become a fullstack Frontend Developer and will be a fully trained web developer by June 2023. I love any collaboration, so don't hesitate to contact me with ideas.
